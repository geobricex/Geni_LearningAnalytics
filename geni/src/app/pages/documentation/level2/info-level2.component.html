Level 2 extends Level 1 by incorporating indicators of student interaction in virtual learning environments. In addition to containing traditional academic information, it allows the evaluation of connection behaviors, forum participation, and resource usage. This structure supports the intersection between performance and digital engagement, providing a more comprehensive view of the educational process.

Level 2 is a list of objects, where each object represents a student with their performance and behavioral data in a specific subject.
  • student_id (str): Unique identifier of the student.
  • student_name (str): Full name.
  • course_id (str): Course code.
  • course_name (str): Course name.
  • accesses_last_7_days (int): Number of times accessed in the last week.
  • total_connection_time_minutes (int): Total accumulated connection time in minutes.
  • resources_visited (int): Total number of visited resources within the virtual classroom.
  • forum_posts (int): Number of forum posts made.
  • activities (list): List of evaluated activities.
  • activity_id (str): Unique identifier of the activity.
  • type (str): Type of activity (e.g., "quiz", "forum").
  • title (str): Title of the evaluative activity.
  • grade (float): Grade obtained in the activity.
  • max_grade (float): Maximum established score.
  • submission_date (str, ISO 8601): Submission date and time.
  • observation (str): Qualitative observation by the teacher regarding performance.
